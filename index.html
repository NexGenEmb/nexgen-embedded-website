Welcome to NexGen Embedded

NexGen Embedded is a pioneering engineering firm specializing in the development of cutting-edge IoT applications using advanced microcontroller technology, specifically the Arduino Uno R3. Our solutions cater to home automation, industrial control, and various other sectors, delivering innovative and efficient embedded systems tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.

Our Services

  • IoT Home Solutions: Smart Home Automation, Energy Management Systems
  • Industrial IoT Solutions: Predictive Maintenance, Process Automation
  • Custom Embedded Systems: Product Development, Prototyping, and Testing
  • Consulting and Support: Technical Consulting, After-Sales Support

Our Mission

To revolutionize the IoT and embedded solutions landscape by providing innovative, high-quality, and reliable services that enhance efficiency, safety, and connectivity for clients in both rural and urban areas.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in embedded technology, driving the future of IoT with innovative and sustainable solutions that transform everyday life and industrial processes.
